## What Works (Makes You Stay, Feels Welcoming)

1. **"Non-competitive" messaging:** Immediately removes anxiety for adults/beginners
2. **Explicit age inclusion:** Names every age group, not just kids
3. **Free trial:** Removes risk of first visit
4. **Community language:** "Family," "safe," "supportive" repeated
5. **Instructor credibility:** Notes they are both skilled dancers AND educators
6. **Diverse styles:** Shows there's something for every interest
7. **Testimonials:** Real parent/student voices (from preview)
8. **Flexibility:** Make-up classes, flexible enrollment removes friction
9. **Local ownership:** 33 years, family-owned (vs. chain feeling)

---

## What Doesn't Work (Confusing, Alienating, Generic)

1. **Limited visual hierarchy clarity:** (Inferred from description) May not be obvious which section to click for your age group on mobile
2. **Neon aesthetic might alienate older users:** Very trendy, could feel "not for me" to 55+ demographic
3. **Hero video may be too fast-cut:** Older users might miss key information
4. **If schedule is hidden:** Being able to see available classes at a glance would be stronger
