## 1. KIDS (5-12 YEARS OLD)

### **How They Discover Classes**
- **Primary:** Word-of-mouth via WhatsApp parent groups (fastest-growing channel)
- **Secondary:** School recommendations, community notice boards
- **Tertiary:** Instagram (via parent searches), local Facebook groups
- **NOT Primary:** Direct Google searches (parents use, not kids)

**Key Finding:** Parents in Malaysia heavily rely on **parent WhatsApp networks** and recommendations from other parents. Parent blogger communities on Facebook are also influential.

### **What Convinces Parents to Sign Up**
1. **Safety & Trust** (highest priority)
   - Certified, professional instructors
   - Parent testimonials & social proof
   - Transparent class structure & curriculum
   - Background checks/credentials prominently displayed

2. **Quality & Program Design**
   - Structured progression levels
   - Performance opportunities (recitals, competitions)
   - Balanced creativity + discipline
   - Multi-style offerings (ballet, modern, K-pop, traditional)

3. **Affordability & Convenience**
   - Trial classes or intro packages
   - Reasonable monthly fees aligned to income level
   - Flexible scheduling (after school, weekends)
   - Location proximity (transportation burden for parents)

4. **Bonding & Family Experience**
   - Parent-child event opportunities
   - Community-building (family socials, showcases)
   - Cultural representation (important in multicultural Malaysia)

### **Pain Points**
- **Cost concerns** (especially B40/M40 families)
- **Time management** (multiple kids, school schedules)
- **Safety worries** (strangers handling children)
- **Distance/transport** (traffic in KL/PJ areas)
- **Language barriers** for non-Malay families
- **Quality consistency** across instructors

### **Malaysia-Specific Insights**
- **Multicultural families value:** Cultural representation (Malay, Chinese, Indian, expat communities)
- **Family importance:** Kids classes often for bonding, not just skill-building
- **School holidays:** Peak enrollment during school breaks
- **Halal/Cultural sensitivities:** Modest dance costumes, prayer time accommodations important for Malay-Muslim families
- **Income diversity:** B40 families may need payment plans; M40/T20 less price-sensitive
