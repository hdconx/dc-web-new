## QUICK CHECKLIST â€” WHAT TO LOOK FOR

As you browse each site, note:

**Visual Hierarchy**
- [ ] Where does your eye go first?
- [ ] Is the most important info at top?
- [ ] Is there a clear flow down the page?

**Color & Contrast**
- [ ] Is text easy to read on dark background?
- [ ] Are photos visible or too dark?
- [ ] What accent colors (if any) stand out?

**Photography/Imagery**
- [ ] How many photos per section?
- [ ] Are they full-bleed or in grids?
- [ ] Are they styled (B&W, color filtered)?
- [ ] Are they real photos or illustrated/AI?

**Whitespace**
- [ ] Does it feel spacious or cramped?
- [ ] Is empty space intentional or accidental?
- [ ] How much padding around sections?

**Typography**
- [ ] Serif or sans-serif headers?
- [ ] How large are the headlines?
- [ ] Is body copy short or long?

**Interactivity**
- [ ] Does anything animate on scroll?
- [ ] Are there hover effects?
- [ ] Is anything interactive (scrollable, filterable)?

**Trust/Credibility**
- [ ] Where do they show social proof?
- [ ] How do they prove they're established?
- [ ] Are testimonials visible?
- [ ] Do they show credentials?

**Call-to-Action**
- [ ] Where's the main CTA button?
- [ ] Is it obvious?
- [ ] How many CTAs total?
