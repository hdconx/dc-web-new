## CATEGORY 1: LUXURY FITNESS BRANDS (Lean INSPIRATIONAL)

### 1. **Equinox** — High-end Fitness Club
- **URL:** https://www.equinox.com/
- **Location:** USA (NY-based, 300+ clubs worldwide)
- **Vibe:** Luxe, minimal, video-first
- **Key Elements:**
  - Dark backgrounds, hover animations
  - Hero video plays automatically
  - Luxury photography, NO labels
  - Messaging: "It's Not Fitness. It's Life"
- **Learn From:** How video + stillness creates rhythm

### 2. **SLT NYC** — Premium Pilates/Reformer
- **URL:** https://sltnyc.com/
- **Location:** USA (NYC-based, multiple locations)
- **Vibe:** Elegant, strength-focused, professional
- **Key Elements:**
  - Clean layouts, power imagery
  - Single strong images
  - Tone: Premium through restraint
  - Messaging: "Strengthen, Lengthen, Tone"
- **Learn From:** How one image can communicate everything

### 3. **Barry's Bootcamp** — HIIT/Boutique
- **URL:** https://www.barrys.com/
- **Location:** USA (multiple locations, expanding)
- **Vibe:** Dark, energetic, bold
- **Key Elements:**
  - Dark red + black aesthetic
  - Confident copy ("The best workout in the world")
  - High-energy photography
  - Studio atmosphere feels dark + energetic
- **Learn From:** Authenticity in messaging + visual confidence

### 4. **Rumble Boxing** — Boutique Boxing
- **URL:** https://www.rumbleboxinggym.com/
- **Location:** Canada/USA (Toronto, multiple locations)
- **Vibe:** Authentic, community-driven, pop-art energy
- **Key Elements:**
  - Philosophy: "Less cheesy fitness B.S., more authenticity"
  - Community + individuality balance
  - Bold personality in copy
  - Dark aesthetic with energetic pops
- **Learn From:** How personality differentiates; "group fitness for the individual"

### 5. **Peloton** — Premium Fitness Tech
- **URL:** https://www.onepeloton.com/
- **Location:** Global (USA-based)
- **Vibe:** Minimalist, personalization-focused, sleek
- **Key Elements:**
  - Minimal design language
  - Personal achievement focus
  - Real people, real moments
  - Tech transparency
- **Learn From:** How simplicity creates delight; micro-moments matter

## CATEGORY 2: DANCE-SPECIFIC STUDIOS (Mix of INSPIRATIONAL & EXPERIENTIAL)

### 6. **CLI Studios** — Pre-Professional Online Dance
- **URL:** https://www.clistudios.com/
- **Location:** USA (Los Angeles, online globally)
- **Vibe:** Sleek black, elite/aspirational, pre-professional
- **Key Elements:**
  - Sleek black aesthetic
  - Messaging: "Global leader in online dance education"
  - Elite positioning, world-renowned choreographers
  - 1000+ classes, instructor profiles
- **Learn From:** How to position aspiration without gatekeeping

### 7. **Arthur Murray** — Ballroom Dance Studios
- **URL:** https://www.arthurmurray.com/
- **Location:** USA (multiple franchise locations worldwide)
- **Vibe:** Warm, accessible, immediate, franchise-professional
- **Key Elements:**
  - Messaging: "You'll be dancing in 5 minutes"
  - Beginner-friendly tone
  - Inclusive, all-ages welcome
  - Fun, approachable aesthetic
- **Learn From:** Immediacy of experience; breaking down barriers

### 8. **DanceWorks Toronto** — Contemporary Dance Company
- **URL:** https://www.danceworks.ca/
- **Location:** Canada (Toronto, performative/educational)
- **Vibe:** Minimalist, artistic, high-quality photography
- **Key Elements:**
  - Sparse, clean layouts
  - High-quality performance imagery
  - Artistic direction, literary tone
  - Trusts the images to speak
- **Learn From:** How minimalism + artistry creates prestige

### 9. **RAW Company** — Contemporary Dance (Australian)
- **URL:** https://rawcompany.net/
- **Location:** Australia
- **Vibe:** 27-year heritage, passion-driven, professional
- **Key Elements:**
  - Messaging: "Driven by passion — to create, teach, inspire"
  - Global reach (performed Off-Broadway)
  - Education + performance blend
  - Clean, artistic presentation
- **Learn From:** Legacy without bragging; passion as driver

### 10. **Centre Stage Dance Academy** — South Asian Dance
- **URL:** https://dancecsda.com/
- **Location:** South Asia/Global
- **Vibe:** Inclusive, multicultural, community-first
- **Key Elements:**
  - Messaging: "Blend of creativity, technique, and heart"
  - Diverse dance styles celebrated
  - All ages, all backgrounds
  - Warm, belonging-focused tone
- **Learn From:** Inclusivity as brand strength; diversity as feature
