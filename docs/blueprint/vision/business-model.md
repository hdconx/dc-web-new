**4 Purpose-Built Studios:**
- Room A: 1,200 sqft (premium, theatrical)
- Room B, C, D: Varying sizes (300-800 sqft)

**Rental Uses:**
- Dance practice/rehearsals
- Fitness classes
- Content creation/video shoots

- Corporate events
- Performances & showcases

**Pricing:**
- Starting from RM 50/hour
- Range: RM 50-120/hour base
- Discounts for longer bookings

## Competitive Advantages

### 1. Multiple Room Options
- 4 purpose-built studios (not converted spaces)
- Range: 300 sqft to 1,200 sqft
- Customers choose based on exact needs and budget

### 2. Affordable & Flexible Pricing
- Starting from RM 50/hour (competitive)
- Substantial discounts for longer bookings
- Negotiable rates for regular renters
- Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)

### 3. Flexible Policies
- Understanding of renter needs
- Accommodating cancellation approach
- One-time reschedule option
- Case-by-case flexibility

### 4. Value-Added Services
- Storage rooms for regular renters
- Equipment available: tripods, lighting, ring lights
- Projector & screen in larger rooms
- Free Wi-Fi throughout
- Free parking after office hours (Mon-Fri after 5pm, all day weekends)

### 5. Renter-Centric Approach
- "Built by dancers for dancers"
- 25+ years understanding the community
- Flexible scheduling (early morning to late night)
- Quick response time (within 2 hours)
- Willing to customize and accommodate

## Business Philosophy

### Core Values
1. **Inclusion** - Dance for everyone, all ages, all backgrounds
2. **Community** - Belonging over competition
3. **Accessibility** - Flexible pricing, scheduling, policies
4. **Authenticity** - Warm, grounded, no marketing fluff
5. **Professionalism** - Quality instruction, maintained facilities

### Key Messages
> "Multiple rooms, flexible pricing, and we understand your needs."
> "This school has classes for people like me."
> "Dance is learned, not inherited."
