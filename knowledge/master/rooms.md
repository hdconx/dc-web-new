## General Notes (All Rooms)

All studio rooms share these characteristics:
- Wall-to-wall or near wall-to-wall mirrors
- Laminated dance-suitable flooring (AC3 HPL)
- Fully air-conditioned
- Supplemented by wall-mounted fans
- Suitable for dance, fitness, and movement-based activities
- Available for hourly, multi-hour, and long-term rental
- Rental time includes setup and cleanup

Each room is an independent rental unit, but some rooms can be used in combination with prior approval.

---

## Room A — Large Studio (Primary Hall)

### Identity
- **Relative Size:** Largest room
- **Primary Role:** Main dance hall / large group studio

Room A is the flagship studio space, designed to accommodate large groups, performances, and high-energy movement sessions.

### Physical Characteristics
- Approximate size: ~1200 square feet
- Open, uninterrupted floor layout
- Three walls with wall-to-wall mirrors
- High ceiling clearance suitable for movement and lifts
- No permanent obstructions on the dance floor

This room offers maximum visual depth, making it ideal for group synchronization, instructor demonstrations, and filming large group sessions.

### Equipment
- High-quality sound system (minimized latency and echo)
- Wireless microphone
- Projector
- Large projection screen

### Climate
- Fully air-conditioned
- Supplemented by six wall-mounted fans
- Designed for high-intensity sessions

### Typical Use Cases
**Primary:**
- Large dance classes
- Fitness programs
- Group rehearsals
- Performance practice
- Dance workshops

**Secondary (with approval):**
- Dance-related social events
- Filming large group content
- Movement-based training events

## Room B — Medium Studio (Flexible Extension)

### Identity
- **Relative Size:** Medium
- **Primary Role:** Flexible studio / overflow space

Room B functions both as a standalone studio and as a supporting extension to Room A when required.

### Physical Characteristics
- Medium-sized studio
- Wall-to-wall high mirrors
- Open layout suitable for instruction and practice
- **Direct connection to Room A**

The adjacency allows:
- Overflow usage
- Instructor prep space
- Breakout or rotation groups

### Equipment
- Sound system
- Air-conditioning
- Wall-mounted fans

### Typical Use Cases
- Medium-sized dance classes
- Private lessons
- Small group rehearsals
- Instructor practice
- Extension of Room A for large events

## Flooring Notes (Critical)

All rooms use AC3 HPL laminated flooring:

**Suitable for:**
- Dance
- Fitness
- Rehearsals

**Balanced grip for:**
- Barefoot
- Sneakers
- Dance shoes (non-marking)

**Critical:** Spills must be strictly avoided due to repair sensitivity.

## Capacity Philosophy

Capacity depends on activity type:
- High-movement dance: Lower capacity for safety
- Low-movement workshops: Higher capacity possible
- Filming: Varies based on equipment needs

Final capacity is subject to:
- Activity intensity
- Instructor supervision
- Approval when exceeding standard expectations

See `/data/rooms.json` for specific capacity numbers by activity type.
