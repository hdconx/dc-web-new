# Target Customer Segments & Renter Personas

> This document identifies all potential renter types and their specific needs.
> Use this to inform website content, room page design, and marketing.

---

## Overview

Dance Connexions serves diverse renter types with varying needs:
- Booking duration (hourly, half-day, recurring, long-term)
- Setup requirements (minimal to complex)
- Equipment needs (basic to full AV)
- Pricing sensitivity (budget to premium)
- Activity type (dance, fitness, content, events)

---

## PRIMARY SEGMENTS (High Volume)

### 1. Dance Instructors & Teachers

**Profile:**
- Independent dance teachers running their own classes
- Dance school instructors needing extra space
- Choreographers developing routines

**Sub-segments:**
| Type | Booking Pattern | Room Preference | Key Needs |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Weekly class instructor | Recurring weekly (same slot) | Room A or D | Consistency, storage, loyalty pricing |
| Freelance teacher | Ad-hoc bookings | Any room | Flexibility, easy booking |
| Guest choreographer | Multi-day intensive | Room A | Large space, projector, long hours |

**Dance Styles Served:**
- Ballet, Contemporary, Jazz
- Hip-hop, Street, K-pop
- Ballroom, Latin, Salsa, Bachata
- Traditional/Cultural (Bharatanatyam, Chinese, Malay)
- Pole fitness, Aerial (conditional)

**What They Value:**
- Mirrors (essential)
- Sound quality (critical for musicality)
- Floor surface (proper dance floor)
- Consistent time slots
- Storage for equipment

**Website Content Needed:**
- Capacity by dance style
- Sound system specs
- Floor type description
- Recurring booking info
- Instructor testimonials

## EMERGING SEGMENTS (Future Growth)

### 9. Audition & Casting Services

**Profile:**
- Talent agencies conducting auditions
- Production companies casting dancers/actors
- Dance companies holding tryouts
- Modeling agencies scouting
- Self-tape recording services

**Global Insight:** In NYC, audition spaces average $43/hour with typical bookings of 3 hours for 6 people. Saturdays are most popular; weekdays can be 43% cheaper. (Source: [Peerspace](https://www.peerspace.com/venues/new-york--ny/audition-space))

**What They Need:**
- Private, professional environment
- Waiting area for candidates
- Recording capability (self-tape)
- Good lighting and acoustics
- Half-day or full-day rates

**Website Content Needed:**
- Audition space setup option
- Waiting area availability
- Self-tape friendly features
- Casting package pricing

## BOOKING PATTERNS SUMMARY

| Pattern | Duration | Typical Customers | Pricing Approach |
|---------|----------|-------------------|------------------|
| **One-off** | 1-3 hours | Creators, auditions, parties | Standard hourly |
| **Half-day** | 4 hours | Workshops, shoots | 15% discount |
| **Full-day** | 8 hours | Productions, intensives | 25% discount |
| **Weekly recurring** | Ongoing | Instructors, fitness | Custom negotiated |
| **Monthly block** | 20+ hrs/month | Schools, academies | Significant discount |
| **Annual contract** | Year commitment | Established instructors | Best rates + storage |

## WEBSITE CONTENT IMPLICATIONS

### Each Room Page Should Show:

1. **Activity Scenarios**
   - "Perfect for..." section with icons
   - Capacity by activity type (not just one number)

2. **Booking Options**
   - Hourly, half-day, full-day rates
   - Recurring booking info
   - Long-term inquiry CTA

3. **Setup Variations**
   - Dance class layout
   - Workshop layout
   - Filming setup
   - Event setup

4. **Equipment for Each Use**
   - Dance: Sound, mirrors, floor
   - Fitness: AC, ventilation, mats
   - Content: Lighting, tripods, power
   - Events: Decoration rules, sound

5. **Testimonials by Type**
   - Dance instructor quote
   - Fitness trainer quote
   - Content creator quote

## FUTURE TIER 3 PROVISIONS

When implementing booking system, account for:

1. **Customer Types**
   - First-time vs returning
   - Instructor vs event organizer
   - Individual vs organization

2. **Booking Complexity**
   - Simple (hourly, no setup)
   - Standard (half-day, basic setup)
   - Complex (multi-room, equipment, custom)

3. **Pricing Rules**
   - Activity-based modifiers
   - Duration discounts
   - Loyalty tiers
   - Peak/off-peak

4. **Cancellation Tiers**
   - Flexible (simple bookings)
   - Standard (most bookings)
   - Strict (complex setups)

5. **Customer Portal Features**
   - Booking history
   - Favorite rooms
   - Saved preferences
   - Loyalty points (future)
