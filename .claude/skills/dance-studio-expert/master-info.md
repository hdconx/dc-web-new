**A1. Business Identity (Rental Context)**
------------------------------------------

**Entity Name:** Dance Connexions\
**Function:** Dance-focused studio rental provider\
**Primary Nature:** Movement-based studio spaces designed for dance,
fitness, and performance-related activities.

Dance Connexions operates multiple mirrored studio rooms within a single
premises. These studios are made available for **short-term and
recurring rentals** primarily to individuals and organizations engaged
in movement-based activities.

This rental operation is **separate** from:

-   Dance Connexions' internal dance class programs

-   Any event-space-branded business entities operating at the same
    > location

**A2. Rental Mission (Canonical Purpose)**
------------------------------------------

The purpose of Dance Connexions studio rentals is to:

-   Monetize unused studio hours efficiently

-   Serve independent instructors, choreographers, performers, and
    > movement professionals

-   Provide safe, functional, and affordable dance-ready spaces

-   Reduce administrative workload through clarity, structure, and
    > self-qualification

**This is not a lifestyle venue.\
**It is a **functional, professional workspace** optimized for movement.

**A3. Primary Intended Use (High Priority)**
--------------------------------------------

The studios are **specifically designed and optimized** for:

### **Core Use Cases (Primary)**

-   Dance classes (all styles)

-   Dance rehearsals

-   Choreography development

-   Fitness classes (yoga, pilates, barre, conditioning)

-   Performance practice

-   Movement-based workshops

-   Private dance coaching

-   Instructor training

These use cases align with:

-   Flooring type

-   Mirror layout

-   Sound system

-   Ventilation

-   Room proportions

**A4. Secondary Acceptable Use (Conditional)**
----------------------------------------------

Certain **non-dance but movement-compatible** activities are allowed
**only if they do not conflict** with studio integrity, noise
environment, or brand positioning.

Examples:

-   Modelling or runway training

-   Acting or performance rehearsals

-   Fitness-related small group training

-   Content creation (photo/video) related to movement or instruction

-   Dance-related social gatherings (e.g. student celebrations, dance
    > parties)

These uses:

-   Must respect studio care rules

-   Must not introduce food, alcohol, or damaging equipment

-   May require prior approval depending on setup

**A5. Explicit Non-Purpose (Exclusions)**
-----------------------------------------

Dance Connexions studios are **not intended for**:

-   Corporate seminars or conferences

-   Banquet-style events

-   Weddings or formal ceremonies

-   Theatre-style seated events

-   Loud parties unrelated to dance or movement

-   Events requiring tables, catering, or external staging

-   Overnight or long-duration non-instructional use

If an inquiry falls under these categories, it is considered **out of
scope** and may be declined.

**A6. Brand Tone (Rental Context)**
-----------------------------------

Rental communications should be:

-   Professional

-   Clear

-   Efficient

-   Respectful

-   Neutral in emotion

Avoid:

-   Overly warm or promotional language

-   Community or "join us" messaging

-   Emotional storytelling (reserved for dance student pages)

**Mindset to convey:**

> "We have a well-equipped studio. If it fits your needs, here's how to
> use it."

**A7. Decision Philosophy (Operational Rule)**
----------------------------------------------

For any rental-related decision, ask:

1.  Is this activity movement-based?

2.  Does it respect the studio floor, mirrors, and equipment?

3.  Does it avoid disruption to other users?

4.  Can it be executed without additional staff involvement?

If **any answer is no**, approval is required or the request may be
declined.
