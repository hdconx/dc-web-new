**Dance Connexions**
====================

**Knowledge Base Management & Supplemental Information Handbook**
-----------------------------------------------------------------

### **(For Humans + AI Agents)**

### 

**1. PURPOSE OF THIS DOCUMENT (READ FIRST)**
--------------------------------------------

### This document exists to ensure that:

-   ### The **Master Knowledge Base (Master KB)** remains stable, authoritative, and trustworthy

-   ### New information can be added **without rewriting or breaking existing content**

-   ### Future AI agents can:

    -   ### Understand context

    -   ### Handle incomplete information

    -   ### Integrate updates safely

    -   ### Avoid hallucinations or contradictions

### **This guide teaches how to THINK about updates --- not just where to paste them.**

### 

**2. KNOWLEDGE BASE ARCHITECTURE (SYSTEM OVERVIEW)**
----------------------------------------------------

### The Dance Connexions rental information system consists of **three layers**:

### **2.1 Master Knowledge Base (Master KB)**

### **Role:** Canonical source of truth **Format:** Structured document (sections A--Q or similar)

### **Characteristics:**

-   ### Stable

-   ### Carefully written

-   ### Reviewed periodically

-   ### Used for:

    -   ### Website

    -   ### Contracts

    -   ### Policies

    -   ### AI grounding

### ⚠️ **Rule: **Do NOT casually edit the Master KB every time new info appears.

### 

### **2.2 Supplemental Knowledge Base (Supplemental KB)**

### **Role:** Intake + staging area for new information **Format:** Log-style document

### **Characteristics:**

-   ### Flexible

-   ### Additive

-   ### Non-destructive

-   ### Can contain:

    -   ### Clarifications

    -   ### Corrections

    -   ### New facilities

    -   ### Operational nuances

    -   ### Exceptions

    -   ### Temporary policies

    -   ### Future ideas

### This is where **most changes go first**.

### 

### **2.3 AI Reasoning Layer (NotebookLM / AI Agents)**

### **Role:** Intelligence and synthesis

### AI agents should:

-   ### Read **both Master KB and Supplemental KB**

-   ### Treat Master KB as authoritative

-   ### Treat Supplemental KB as **contextual override or expansion**

-   ### Never invent rules not found in either

### 

**3. WHY SUPPLEMENTAL INFORMATION EXISTS**
------------------------------------------

### New information often:

-   ### Is incomplete

-   ### Affects multiple sections

-   ### Is not yet finalized

-   ### Might change again

-   ### Was shared informally (chat, call, WhatsApp)

### If we immediately merge this into the Master KB, we risk:

-   ### Contradictions

-   ### Rewrites

-   ### Inconsistencies

-   ### Loss of historical context

### **Therefore:**

### All new information enters the system via the Supplemental KB first.

### 

**4. WHAT COUNTS AS "SUPPLEMENTAL INFORMATION"**
------------------------------------------------

### Examples include (but are not limited to):

-   ### Clarifications (e.g. parking nuance by time)

-   ### Operational flexibility (e.g. rental hours unrestricted)

-   ### Newly available equipment

-   ### Corrections to assumptions

-   ### Temporary arrangements

-   ### Edge cases

-   ### Capacity explanations

-   ### Differences between dance vs non-dance usage

-   ### New contact details

-   ### Additional advantages (mirrors, lighting control, Wi-Fi)

### 

**5. HOW TO STRUCTURE THE SUPPLEMENTAL KB**
-------------------------------------------

### **5.1 Recommended Format**

### Each entry should follow this pattern:

### **SUPPLEMENTAL ENTRY ID:** S-YYYY-MM-DD-XX **Related Master Section(s):** (e.g. Section D, F, J) **Status:** Temporary / Pending / Confirmed **Type:** Clarification / Addition / Correction / Expansion

### **Content: **Clear, plain-language description of the information.

### **Notes: **Context, rationale, or future integration hints.

### 

### **5.2 Example Entry**

### **SUPPLEMENTAL ENTRY ID:** S-2025-01-14-01 **Related Sections:** Facilities, Parking, Booking **Status:** Confirmed **Type:** Clarification

### **Content: **Parking is available via municipal street parking (MBSJ rates approx. RM0.50/hour during office hours). After office hours, weekends, and public holidays, parking around the premises is free and can accommodate large crowds (40--60 vehicles).

### **Notes: **Avoid claiming "ample parking during office hours" to prevent neighbour impact. Emphasize evening/night convenience instead.

### 

**6. HOW AI AGENTS SHOULD USE THIS SYSTEM**
-------------------------------------------

### **6.1 Priority Rules**

1.  ### Master KB overrides everything

2.  ### Supplemental KB refines or expands Master KB

3.  ### If conflict exists:

    -   ### Report the conflict

    -   ### Prefer the **newer supplemental entry**

4.  ### If information is missing:

    -   ### Ask for clarification

    -   ### Do NOT invent details

### 

### **6.2 When Generating Content (Website, WhatsApp, Contracts)**

### AI should:

-   ### Extract only **relevant sections**

-   ### Match tone to use case:

    -   ### Website → professional, concise

    -   ### WhatsApp → friendly, operational

    -   ### Terms → firm, protective

-   ### Never paste full policies unless required

-   ### Prefer summaries unless full text is explicitly requested

### 

**7. INTEGRATION POLICY (WHEN TO UPDATE MASTER KB)**
----------------------------------------------------

### Do **NOT** integrate supplemental info immediately.

### Integration should happen when:

-   ### Information has stabilized

-   ### No contradictions remain

-   ### It affects multiple outputs consistently

-   ### The owner confirms it is "final enough"

### **Suggested cadence:**

-   ### Review Supplemental KB every 1--3 months

-   ### Batch-integrate into Master KB

-   ### Increment version number (e.g. v1.1 → v1.2)

### 

**8. VERSIONING & TRACEABILITY**
--------------------------------

### **Master KB**

-   ### Versioned (v1.0, v1.1, v1.2...)

-   ### Changelog maintained

### **Supplemental KB**

-   ### Append-only

-   ### Never delete entries

-   ### Superseded entries should be marked, not removed

### This ensures:

-   ### Auditability

-   ### AI consistency

-   ### Historical reasoning

### 

**9. IMAGE & MEDIA PLACEHOLDER RULES**
--------------------------------------

### Until real images/videos are provided:

-   ### Use **clearly labeled placeholders**

-   ### Never assume visual details

-   ### AI should suggest image types, not fabricate visuals

### Example:

### "Placeholder image: Studio A -- large mirrored dance space with controlled lighting"

### 

**10. LEGAL & POLICY CONTENT HANDLING**
---------------------------------------

### Terms & Conditions should exist in **tiers**:

-   ### Friendly summary (website)

-   ### Operational rules (rental info)

-   ### Full legal version (contracts)

### AI must:

-   ### Select the appropriate tier

-   ### Avoid overly harsh language unless explicitly requested

-   ### Never shorten legal text without permission

###

### With this structure, you can:

-   ### Add AI agents safely

-   ### Scale content across platforms

-   ### Maintain consistency

-   ### Reduce admin overhead

-   ### Avoid knowledge loss

-   ### Evolve without chaos

### This is **knowledge operations**, not just documentation.

### 

**12. FINAL PRINCIPLE (MOST IMPORTANT)**
----------------------------------------

### **Never sacrifice clarity for speed. Never sacrifice structure for convenience. Never sacrifice truth for marketing.**

### The system exists to protect both **operations and reputation**.
