# Studios & Rooms

Dance Connexions has **4 distinct studio rooms**, each suited for different needs.

## Room Overview

| Room | Name | Size | Base Rate | Best For |
|------|------|------|-----------|----------|
| A | Main Space | 1,200 sqft | RM 120/hr | Large groups, workshops, events |
| D | Creation Studio | 700 sqft | RM 80/hr | Medium classes, content creation |
| B | Intimate Studio | 400 sqft | RM 60/hr | Small groups, connected to A |
| C | Workshop Space | 300 sqft | RM 50/hr | Private practice, budget option |

## Room A - Main Space (Large Studio)

**The flagship studio** - largest and most versatile.

### Physical Specs
- **Size:** 1,200 sq ft (111.5 sq m)
- **Mirrors:** Wall-to-wall on 3 walls
- **Floor:** Sprung wooden dance floor
- **Climate:** Air-conditioned + 6 ceiling fans
- **Lighting:** Controllable LED lighting

### Capacity Guidelines
| Activity Type | Comfortable | Maximum |
|---------------|-------------|---------|
| Dance Class | 40 | 50 |
| Workshop | 30 | 40 |
| Fitness | 25 | 35 |
| Event | 60 | 80 |
| Filming | 10 | 20 |

### Equipment
- **Included:** Professional sound system, wireless mic, basic lighting
- **Available:** Projector, screen, additional mics, tripods

### Best Use Cases
- Large group dance classes (all styles)
- Workshops and masterclasses
- Dance rehearsals and showcases
- Fitness boot camps
- Large-scale content creation

## Floor Care Rules

To protect our dance floors:
- ❌ No outdoor shoes
- ❌ No high heels that may damage floor
- ❌ No food/drinks on dance floor
- ✅ Dance shoes or clean indoor footwear only
- ✅ Water bottles with secure lids (off the floor)
