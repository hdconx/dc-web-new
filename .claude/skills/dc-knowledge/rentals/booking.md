## Booking Flow

```
1. Inquiry → 2. Quote → 3. Deposit → 4. Confirmation → 5. Rental
```

### Step 1: Inquiry
Contact us with:
- Preferred date(s)
- Time slot (start & end time)
- Room preference (or let us recommend)
- Activity type
- Approximate group size

### Step 2: Quote
We'll respond with:
- Availability status
- Price breakdown
- Any applicable discounts
- Alternative options (if first choice unavailable)

### Step 3: Deposit
- **Amount:** 50% of total
- **Purpose:** Confirms your booking
- **Methods:** Bank transfer, online payment, cash

### Step 4: Confirmation
Once deposit received:
- Booking is confirmed
- You'll receive confirmation message
- Calendar is blocked for your slot

### Step 5: Rental Day
- Arrive on time (or slightly early)
- Pay remaining balance
- Enjoy your session!

## Booking Guidelines

### Minimum Booking
- **1 hour** minimum per session

### Advance Booking
- **Recommended:** 48+ hours in advance
- **Last-minute:** Subject to availability
- **Recurring:** Can book weekly slots in advance

### Operating Hours
- **Earliest:** 6:00 AM
- **Latest:** 12:00 AM (midnight)
- Flexible scheduling within these hours

## On the Day

### Arrival
- Arrive 5-10 minutes early
- Check in with staff (if present)
- Pay remaining balance

### During Your Session
- Respect your booked time slot
- Follow floor care rules
- Keep noise reasonable (especially late hours)

### End of Session
- End on time (next booking may follow)
- Leave space tidy
- Report any issues

## Overtime Policy

If you need to extend beyond your booked time:
- **Check availability** first (another booking may follow)
- **Overtime rate:** 1.5x normal rate
- Must be agreed in advance when possible

## Cancellation & Changes

### Cancellation Policy
| Notice Given | Refund |
|--------------|--------|
| 48+ hours before | 100% refund |
| 24-48 hours before | 50% refund |
| Less than 24 hours | No refund |

*Credit notes may be issued at management discretion*

### Rescheduling
- Free rescheduling with 48+ hours notice
- Subject to availability
- Same policies apply to rescheduled date

### No-Show
- Treated as last-minute cancellation
- No refund
- Repeated no-shows may affect future bookings

## Recurring Bookings

For regular weekly bookings:
- **Discount:** 15% off for 4+ bookings/month
- **Priority:** Your slot is reserved weekly
- **Flexibility:** Can skip with notice (no charge)
- **Payment:** Weekly or monthly arrangements available

## Group & Corporate Bookings

For organizations, schools, or large groups:

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I view the studio before booking?**
A: Yes! Contact us to arrange a viewing during admin hours.

**Q: Can I book for someone else?**
A: Yes, just provide the user's contact details.

**Q: What if I need to leave early?**
A: The booked time is yours - no refund for unused time.

**Q: Can I store equipment at the studio?**
A: Not typically, but ask about special arrangements.
