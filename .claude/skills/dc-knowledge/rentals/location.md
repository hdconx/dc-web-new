## How to Get Here

### From LDP (Lebuhraya Damansara-Puchong)
1. Exit at Sunway/PJS 11
2. Turn into Jalan PJS 11/2
3. Look for our signage on the 2nd floor

### From Federal Highway
1. Take the Sunway exit
2. Follow signs to Bandar Sunway
3. Navigate to Jalan PJS 11/2

### Landmarks
- Near Sunway Pyramid (5-10 min drive)
- Above commercial shops row
- Look for Dance Connexions signage on 2nd floor

## Parking

### During Office Hours (Mon-Fri 8am-6pm)
- **Type:** Street parking (MBSJ managed)
- **Rate:** ~RM 0.50/hour
- **Availability:** Limited (shared with other businesses)

### After Hours, Weekends & Public Holidays
- **Rate:** FREE
- **Availability:** Ample parking
- **Capacity:** 40-60 vehicles can be accommodated

### Recommendation
For large groups or events, book during evenings, weekends, or public holidays for free parking.

## Public Transport

- **Nearest LRT/MRT:** Subang Jaya (10-15 min by Grab)
- **By Grab/Taxi:** Use address or share Google Maps location
