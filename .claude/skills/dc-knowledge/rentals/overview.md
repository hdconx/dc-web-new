# Dance Connexions - Business Overview

## Identity

**Dance Connexions** is a premium dance studio and rental facility located in Bandar Sunway, Petaling Jaya, Malaysia. Established in **1999**, we've served the dance community for over 25 years.

**Tagline:** "The Movement Ecosystem"

## Core Business

### Primary Service: Studio Rentals
Professional dance studio spaces available for hourly rental:
- 4 distinct studios (Rooms A, B, C, D)
- Sizes from 300 to 1,200 sq ft
- Fully equipped with mirrors, sound, AC
- Flexible scheduling from early morning to late midnight

### Secondary Service: Dance Classes
Regular dance classes for all ages (to be expanded in Phase 2):
- Kids (5-12)
- Teens (13-17)
- Young Adults (18-25)
- Adults (26-49)
- Active Adults (50+)

## Target Customers

### Primary (Rentals)
1. **Dance Instructors** - Teaching their own classes
2. **Dance Groups** - Rehearsals and practice
3. **Content Creators** - TikTok, YouTube, social media
4. **Fitness Instructors** - Dance fitness, yoga, etc.
5. **Choreographers** - Preparing performances

### Secondary
1. **Photographers** - Dance photography sessions
2. **Event Organizers** - Dance-focused private events
3. **Schools/Companies** - Team building, workshops

## What We're NOT

Dance Connexions is **NOT** for:
- General event venues (birthday parties without dance focus)
- Conference rooms
- Commercial filming (without prior approval)
- Activities that may damage the floor (heels, equipment)

## Brand Tone

- **Professional** but approachable
- **Welcoming** to all skill levels
- **Focused** on movement and dance
- **Flexible** in accommodating needs
- **Transparent** in pricing and policies

## Competitive Advantage

1. **Established reputation** - 25+ years serving the community
2. **Multiple room options** - From intimate to large spaces
3. **Flexible pricing** - Various discounts and packages
4. **Prime location** - Easy access, free evening parking
5. **Well-maintained facilities** - Clean, professional spaces
