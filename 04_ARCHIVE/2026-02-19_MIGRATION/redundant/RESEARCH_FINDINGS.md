## Key Patterns Found: Emotional Flow

### What Works (Creates "Yes" Moments)
âœ… **Dark, premium backgrounds** (black, zinc-950, dark gray)  
   - Equinox, Rumble, CLI, Barry's all use this  
   - Communicates luxury + focus  

âœ… **One hero image/video** (no captions, speaks for itself)  
   - Equinox: video-first approach  
   - SLT: single powerful image of movement  
   - Creates pause + discovery, not density  

âœ… **Asymmetrical spacing** (not grid-like)  
   - Breathing room between sections  
   - Text breaks, whitespace as design element  
   - Feels intimate, not institutional  

âœ… **Typography as hierarchy**  
   - Large, confident headlines  
   - Short copy (Rumble: "less cheesy B.S.")  
   - First-person language or direct emotion  

âœ… **NO section labels/headers**  
   - Equinox doesn't say "Our Facilities"  
   - Flows naturally, feels discovered not listed  

âœ… **Photography that shows *feeling*, not just activity**  
   - Movement captured mid-expression  
   - Faces, emotion, energy visible

âœ… **Copy that speaks to transformation**  
   - Rumble: "Group fitness for the individual"
